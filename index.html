My Hobbies

Dancing

One of my main hobbies is dancing. I am interested in improving my skills and it makes me the happiest. It helps me build more confidence and create more friends.

Acting

I love acting! I only discovered this skill of mine when I was assigned to do the declamation monologue in our school. At first, I didn't like it because it made me laugh, but when I focused on my acting, I gained more confidence.

It helps me express my real feelings and emotions. It improves my creativity.

Leadership

I am happy when I have responsibilities. Whenever I accomplish it, it feels like I have another reason to be proud of myself.

It makes me feel like I did amazing.
